La Habana

La Habana is a city of more than two million habitants. It is the capital of Cuba, the biggest island in the Caribbean region. La Habana recently celebrated its five houndred aniersary of being foundation. The most touristic part of the city is La Habana Vieja, the oldest part of the city. In colonial times, La Habana wad a wall that protected the city from the attack of pirates. Nowadays, you could only see the remains of that structure.
La Habana is famous for hosting a huge collection of antique U.S. cars, from the 1940’s and 1950’s. All these cars are still working. Appart from the cars, tourist would get fascinated seing all the diffetent arquitecture its the buildings. ArtDeco arquitecture is one of the favorite of many people. One of the most iconic simbols of the city is the Capitol, use to host the Chanber of Representatives, now it is a museum that was recently renovated.
